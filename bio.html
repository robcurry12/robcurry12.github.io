Robert Curry

"Jack of all trades, master of none, though oftentimes better than master of one"

I am currently working towards a Bachelors of Science in computer science and mathematics at Dowling College. May of 2016 is my anticipated date of graduation. I am forecasted to graduate with a cumulative GPA of 3.8 and a computer science cumulative GPA of 3.7.

The coursework throughout my time at Dowling College as exposed me to a smorgasbord of programming languages, techniques, and styles. I have worked closely and intensely with the some of the biggest web technologies such as JQuery, PHP, and HTML/CSS. The "Portfolio" section of this website offers samples of my work that you can check out.

Aside from academics, I have also been employed at the Dowling College Help Desk as a Work Study technician for the last four years. In my spare time, I occasionally will write articles for a fan run New York Mets fan-blog called The Rising Apple Blog at Fansided.com. I am strictly a volunteer writer and write purely for pleasure. I also have a musical side that is expressed through my Youtube Channel. I post drum covers of popular songs for entertainment and constructive criticism. I have also experimented with audio and video editing with my drum covers as well as other Youtube projects.

Achieving high grades while involving myself in a variety of hobbies and interests was made possible by my excellent time and stress management skills. These are skills I utilize in all facets of my life to help keep things light for myself and others around me. I understand the importance of hustling, hardworking, and perservering through adversity. However, I also understand the importance of relaxation and knowing your limits. To be successful in business and in life, one must balance these two ideas effectively.

My eclectic collection of experiences, hobbies, and interests is what sets me apart from others. I have developed a unique mindset by being involved in these different areas of concentration. I bring a multidimensional school of thought to the table which is crucial in life and especially in computer science where problem solving is key. These are traits that I pride myself on and feel that I excel at. To wrap things up, when you engage Rob Curry, it is a pleasant, unique, one-of-a-kind experience. I guarantee it!
