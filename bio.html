Robert Curry

"Jack of all trades, master of none, though oftentimes better than master of one"

The coursework throughout my time at Dowling College has exposed me to a smorgasbord of programming languages, techniques, and styles. I have worked closely and intensely with the some of the biggest web technologies such as JQuery, PHP, and HTML/CSS. The "Portfolio" section of this website offers samples of my work that you can check out.

Currently, I am employed at Artbean Inc, an E-commerce start-up company as an IT Specialist/Web Developer. Aside from providing technical support to the office staff, I am also deeply involved with the website development process. This position has exposed me to not only the web developement process but it has allowed me to be deeply immersed in the marketing, SEO/SEM, and the backend architeture aspects of the website.

In my spare time, I work on coding personal side projects for personal consumption, some of which can be found at my Github. Coding in my free time is one of my favorite past times as it allows me to get creative. I also have a musical side that is expressed through my Youtube Channel. I post drum covers of popular songs for entertainment and constructive criticism. I have also experimented with audio and video editing with my drum covers as well as other Youtube projects.
